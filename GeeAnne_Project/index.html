Hi, I'm Gee Anne

Hi, I'm Gee-Anne, a 3rd- year Nursing Student from Cebu Sacred Heart College. Since I was a child, I always liked to be exploring and wanting to see different places. Travelling has been one of my hobbies ever since then. I love how it let you experience new cultures, different sights and learn more about the world.

Here on this blog, I am documenting the places I have been with my friends, family and boyfriend. These trips made me the person I am today and further developed my passion for traveling. My blog has a lot of information about traveling but also some travel stories that I wrote while traveling.

Bohol

Beautiful beaches, marvelous marine life, and the delightful Chocolate Hills is unique geological formations of over 1000 cone-shaped hills that change in color depending on the season.

Palawan

Coron is the northern part of Palawan. It is the third largest island. It has caught the attention of many due to its World War II wreck diving spots, beautiful rock formations, and unbelievably clear waters.
